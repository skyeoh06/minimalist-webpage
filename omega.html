Omega-3 Fatty Acids

Health Benefits:

  • Alpha-linolenic acid (ALA)-produce energy for cells
  • Eicosapentaenoic acid (EPA)- anti-inflammatory
  • Docosahexaenoic acid (DHA)-proper function of our nervous system
  • Prevention of cardiovascular diseases

Food Source:

  • Flaxseeds, Walnuts, Mustard Seeds
  • Sardines, Salmon, Beef, Shrimp, Cod
  • Brussels Sprouts, Cauliflower, Winter Squash, Broccoli, Collard Greens, Spinach, Summer Squash, Kale, Romaine Lettuce, Turnip Greens, Bok Choy
  • Raspberries, Strawberries
  • Green Beans, Miso
  • Basil, Leeks
